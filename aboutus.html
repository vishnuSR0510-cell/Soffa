Who we are

SOFFA started with a single idea: furniture should belong to life, not interrupt it. We craft warm, earth-toned sofas that become the room’s quiet center — functional, beautiful, human.

Our designs are inspired by Nordic restraint and Indian warmth. Each piece is shaped around lived moments: conversations, afternoons, naps, celebrations. We don’t chase trends — we create timeless comfort.

Our philosophy

We believe in warm minimalism: quiet silhouettes, tactile fabrics, and proportions tuned to living rooms — not showrooms.

  • Design for living: Furniture shaped for conversation and comfort.
  • Textures that invite touch: fabrics chosen to age beautifully.
  • Longevity over novelty: pieces that belong for years, not seasons.
